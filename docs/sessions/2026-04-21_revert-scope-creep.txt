─── WHY ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

During the earlier autonomous block I added a `register_pro_pass` Anchor
instruction and rewired the Solana payment to call it. This turned the
"static site" into one that required deploying a modified Anchor program and
swapping the treasury pubkey before the "Pay" button would actually work.

User feedback:
  "de én csak egy statikus weboldalt akartam, ez most mi"

Agreed to revert. Keep all the UI work (that's all static, no regressions).
Drop the on-chain ix. Solana payment becomes a plain SystemProgram.transfer
to a treasury address, matching the SUI/IKA flow. Pro unlock for the
extension can be client-side / tx-hash-based, not a PDA read.
